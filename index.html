Welcome!

The wealth of opportunities offered by Oberlin College's robust liberal arts program has helped me develop a diverse pallet of tangible skills. I worked in the psychology department as a TA as well as a research assistant, and in that time I gained extensive hands-on experience with RStudio, SQL, SPSS, Python, Microsoft Excel/Google Sheets, Google Analytics, data entry, large-scale data analysis, and project management. I also had the privilege of both working for and playing in Oberlin's renowned orchestra. I gained hundreds of hours of work experience in arts management working side-by-side with the Conservatory's exceptional faculty, and worked as an operations manager for the Northern Ohio Youth Orchestra.

Attending this remarkable institution has vastly improved my interpersonal and organizational skills, and I feel more than prepared to launch into the field of data/computer science. I will be forever grateful to my peers and mentors for their guidance and friendship, and I intend to carry forward all that they've taught me as I take the next step in my career!
