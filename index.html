Welcome!

Thanks to the wealth of opportunities offered by Oberlin College's robust liberal arts program, I have spent the last 5 years developing a diverse pallet of skills in many different domains. I worked in the psychology department as a TA as well as a research assistant, and in that time gained extensive hands-on experience with RStudio, SQL, SPSS, Python, Microsoft Excel/Google Sheets, Google Analytics, data entry, large-scale data analysis, and project management. I also had the privilege of both working for and playing in Oberlin's renowned orchestra. I gained hundreds of hours of work experience in arts management working side-by-side with the Conservatory's exceptional faculty, and worked as an operations manager for the Northern Ohio Youth Orchestra.

Attending this remarkable institution has vastly improved my interpersonal and organizational skills, and I will be forever grateful to my peers and mentors for their guidance and friendship. I intend to carry forward all they've taught me as I launch into the next step of my career!
